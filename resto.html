Top Tables Across Italy!

- Osteria Francescana :


Location: Modena, Emilia-Romagna



view: Minimalist interior focused on art and plating, no outdoor view
Best plate: “Five Ages of Parmigiano Reggiano” – a tasting of Parmesan textures
style: Modern Italian fine dining with avant-garde creativity






- La Pergola :


Location: Rome, Lazio

view:Panoramic terrace overlooking Rome’s skyline
Best plate: Fagotelli “La Pergola” – pasta filled with carbonara sauce
style: Refined Mediterranean cuisine with global influences






- Ristorante Grotta Palazzese :


Location: Polignano a Mare, Puglia



view:Set inside a seaside cave with dramatic Adriatic views
Best plate: Lobster tagliolini with citrus zest
style: Coastal fine dining with romantic ambiance





- Da Vittorio :


Location: Brusaporto, Lombardy



view:Surrounded by lush gardens and countryside
Best plate: Paccheri alla Vittorio – signature tomato and Parmesan pasta
style: Gourmet Lombard cuisine with family-run warmth






- Pizzeria Gino Sorbillo :


Location: Naples, Campania



view:Historic street scenes in Naples’ old town
Best plate: Margherita pizza with San Marzano tomatoes and buffalo mozzarella
style: Traditional Neapolitan pizzeria with lively atmosphere
